How it works

A real engine, mirrored in the browser.

The implementation is a Python package: skincare/parser.py parses and normalises the list and resolves INCI names and synonyms; skincare/analyzer.py annotates each ingredient and builds a skin-type-aware report. It ships with a pytest suite and a demo.py. This page fetches the very same data/ingredients.json and reproduces the same rules client-side so the site needs no server.

Skin-type rules: acne-prone flags comedogenic ≥ 3/5; sensitive flags fragrance / essential oils and other irritants/allergens; dry flags strong surfactants, exfoliants and denatured alcohol; oily notes heavy occlusives; pregnancy flags pregnancy-caution actives such as retinoids. Comedogenic ratings are approximate and context-dependent.

Educational tool, not medical advice. Always patch-test new products and consult a professional for medical concerns.
